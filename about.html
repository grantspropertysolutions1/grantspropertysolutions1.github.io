Our Story

Grant's Property Solutions LLC was founded by William Mark Grant Jr with a simple but powerful mission: to give property owners in the Triangle area access to honest, professional, and reliable property services — all from one trusted team.

With over 10 years of hands-on industry experience, William has managed 100+ residential properties and maintained a 98% client retention rate. The LLC was formally established in September 2025 (SOSID 3131762) to scale and structure what was previously operated through individual engagements.

We believe your property is one of your most valuable assets. That's why we treat every job — whether a quick repair or a full renovation — with the same level of care, documentation, and follow-through.
